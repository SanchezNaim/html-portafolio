About my school

I'm currently studying in the state of Veracruz in the fourth semester of what the SEP refers to "upper secondary education", the equivalent to ten to twelve grade in the EU and Canada. The school I am studying at is the CETIS 15 (Centro de Estudios Tecnologicos, Industriales y de Servicios numero 15). The school is a technical one, which means that besides the main subjects you'll have there like maths and physics, you'll also have a main module of one of the actual 5 careers the school offers to the students which are Accounting, Programming, Electricity, Construction, and Human Resources. So, when you finish your studies there, besides your certification of the school, you also get a technical degree certification in your module.
